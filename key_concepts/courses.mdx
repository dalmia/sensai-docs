---
title: Courses
---

You can manage your courses from the `Courses` tab in the `Admin Panel`. Courses consist of [Tasks](/key_concepts/tasks) grouped into [Milestones](/key_concepts/milestones) and assigned to [Cohorts](/key_concepts/cohorts).
